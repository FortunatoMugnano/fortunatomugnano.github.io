Enter Q!

With this application, users can track job searches in a user-friendly and organized way. The home page has five sections where users can drag and move the Job's Card around them. Entity and Identity frameworks were used for the back-end creation of the Database pipeline, along with C# and ADO.Net Core. The client's side was made using React.Js. Styling library was Bootstrap and React-Select.

Sapori D'Italia

An open source Italian-recipes App. Users can search from all the 20 regions of Italy and save their favorite Italian recipes in their personal cookbooks. They can also contribute with their personal recipes, adding the new ones in the database where other users can search, rate and save them.
